Let's talk about what the journey actually looks like.

There's the trying-to-conceive stage — when your body feels like a mystery, and every article about "boosting fertility naturally" either oversimplifies or overwhelms. Hormones, cycles, nutrition, stress: all connected, rarely explained together.

Then there's pregnancy itself. The changes come fast. Your body shifts in ways you weren't prepared for. Some days you feel wonder. Some days you feel confusion, worry, or a strange sadness that no one warned you about.

And after? The postpartum period can feel like standing in unfamiliar territory. The conversations about "bouncing back" feel disconnected from your actual experience.

None of this is weakness. It's what happens when women don't have access to clear, compassionate, evidence-informed guidance from the very beginning.
